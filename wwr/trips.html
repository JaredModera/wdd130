Trips!

Family Rafting

Family Rafting is a slow water rafting with lots of fauna and flora to admire! As a family, groups from 3 up to 7, are available from ages 8 - 69. Approximately 1/2h - 2h (hours) long, with professional guides the whole time.

Flash Rafting

Flash rafting is a fast water rafting with lots of adrenaline and fun! With extreme velocity, it gives you the opportunity to achieve personal best records. Groups from 2 up to 6 people. Available for ages 16 - 65. Approximately 1h (hours) - 45min long, with professional guides the whole time.

Heavy Rafting

Heavy rafting is an extreme experience to enjoy only if you dare to try it! With many obstacles and even jumps, Heavy rafting is only available for the ages of 18 - 50 (minor with the supervision of an adult). Groups from 1 (plus the guide) up to 4 people. Approximately 20min - 35min long, with professional guides the whole time.
