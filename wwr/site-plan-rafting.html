Porpuse

As a family, here in Dry Oar Rafting, we want to give you the best safe and fun, Rafting experience. If you want to enjoy a quality family time or just to have fun by your self, this is the perfect place for you!

Audience

Families and individuals who are looking for a vocational attraction in the age range of 12-69. If you want a personalized experience, we offer you different plans in order to satisfy you needs. Wanting to have a family lunch? or dinner? We offer you a menu full of all our delicious plates and even fast food for your own pleasure. You have any safety issues? Don`t worry, we have the best materials and conditions for our boats, including secure equipment and regular inspection of the rafting zones so they won`t cause any trouble, and in case of an emergency we always have a professional medical assistant ready to help.
